Information We Collect

Section 02

We believe in data minimization. The App is designed to allow you to scan and identify antiques without creating an account or providing personal details.

1. Personal Data

We do NOT collect any directly identifying personal details (such as your name, email address, physical address, phone number, or contact list).

2. Non-Personal & Technical Data

We collect certain technical data automatically to support app stability, performance, and ads monetization:

📷

Camera & Gallery Data

Section 03

The core feature of the App is identifying vintage items from photographs. To do this, the App requests permission to access your device's Camera and/or local Storage.

How We Process Images:
- When you take a photo using the camera or select one from your gallery, the image is cropped and resized locally on your device.
- To identify the item, the image is analyzed using AI models and search algorithms. During search operations, images may be temporarily uploaded to a file hosting service (e.g. https://tmpfiles.org) so that visual indexing API calls can inspect them.
- Images uploaded this way are temporary and subject to automated deletion. We do not store or keep images on our own servers, and we do not use your photos to build database profiles of you.

Data Storage & Retention

Section 05

Your antique collections, custom category folders, and search history are saved locally on your device's internal SQLite database (via Room Database).

  • No history or collection data is uploaded to our servers.
  • If you uninstall the App or clear the App's data in Android System Settings, all your saved collections, items, and scan history will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.
