The App processes images using Artificial Intelligence (AI) algorithms, neural network image recognition, and public web resources to attempt to identify vintage items.

App Limitations & Known Exceptions

Section 11

By using the App you acknowledge the following known limitations and exceptions:

Identification Limitations

  • Poor photo quality: Blurry, dark, or low-resolution photos may produce inaccurate or no results. Results quality is directly dependent on photo quality.
  • Common or modern items: The App is optimized for antiques and vintage objects. Scanning modern, mass-produced, or generic items may produce incorrect or irrelevant results.
  • Web screenshot inputs: Scanning a screenshot, digital image, or photo of a photo (rather than the physical object) may produce degraded or misleading results. The App is not designed or optimized for such inputs.
  • Partial or obscured views: Items that are partially visible, damaged, heavily worn, or photographed at difficult angles may be incorrectly identified.
  • Highly similar items: When many similar antiques exist (e.g., common porcelain patterns, mass-produced pottery), the App may display information for a related but distinct item.
